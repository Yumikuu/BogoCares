Frequently Asked Questions

To add a new donor manually:

  1. Navigate to the Donors page
  2. Click the "Add New Donor" button
  3. Fill in the donor's information in the form
  4. Click "Save Donor" to add them to your database

The donor will now appear in your donor list and you can track their donations.

To export donation data:

  1. Go to the Donations page
  2. Use the filters to narrow down the data you want to export (optional)
  3. Click the "Export" button in the top right of the donations table
  4. Choose your preferred format (CSV, Excel, or PDF)

The file will download to your computer with all the selected donation data.

To enable recurring donations:

  1. Go to Settings → Payment Settings
  2. Ensure that "Enable Recurring Donations" is turned on
  3. Configure your Stripe or PayPal integration for recurring payments
  4. Set up the donation intervals (monthly, quarterly, annually)

Once enabled, donors will see the option to make recurring donations on your donation forms.

To customize donation receipts:

  1. Navigate to Settings → General Settings
  2. Scroll to the "Receipt Template" section
  3. Customize the template with your organization's logo, message, and contact information
  4. Use the preview feature to see how receipts will look to donors
  5. Save your changes

All future donation receipts will use your customized template.

To integrate with MailChimp:

  1. Go to Settings → Integrations
  2. Find the MailChimp section and toggle the switch to enable it
  3. Enter your MailChimp API key and Audience ID
  4. Configure which donor information should sync to MailChimp
  5. Test the connection to ensure it's working properly

Once integrated, new donors will automatically be added to your MailChimp audience.
